ALARM RESPONSE SECURITY GUARDS

A study found that installing a security system in your home alone can cut your risk of burglary by as much as 60%. However, by utilising the system and benefiting from our monitoring service, you may safeguard your family, home, or place of business in the event of an emergency.

WHY SHOULD A SECURITY ALARM SYSTEM BE INSTALLED?

These systems keep your business secure against break-ins and thefts, loss or damage of stock and property, and a wide range of other security benefits, like ...

  • Protection against intrusion and burglary
  • Attract customers to a safer environment
  • Identification of biosecurity threats using thermal imaging
  • Remote monitoring

ALARM MONITORING

The 24-hour-a-day, quick, and trustworthy alarm monitoring team of Inforce Security is on call. Our team will be there in a couple of minutes if your alarm goes off while you are out of town. Each alert reaction gives a thorough report with suggestions where necessary. You'll be informed as to what set off the alarm and what our rapid response team found at the location.
